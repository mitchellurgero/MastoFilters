Mastodon Regular Expression Generator!

This RegEx generator will generate a working word filter for Mastodon allowing you to better filter against words that you do not like or just dont want to see.

To use this filter:

  1. Add your words to the text area below. Make sure you have only ONE word per line!!!
  2. Click Generate Now~ to get the filter in the area below the text area (marked).
  3. In the settings for the timeline you want to filter (Public, home, federated, notification) paste the new RegEx into the filter.
